An Eclipse

Last year, something very special happened in my town. It was a solar eclipse! The sun and the moon were in the sky, and many people were outside watching.

The sky was clear, and the weather was good. While we were waiting for the eclipse, the sun was shining and birds were singing. Everyone was looking at the sky with special glasses.

Suddenly, the sky started to change. The moon slowly moved in front of the sun. It was getting darker and colder. People were talking and taking pictures. Some kids were asking questions because they didn’t understand what was happening.

For a few minutes, the moon covered the sun completely. It wasn’t night, but it was very dark. It felt strange. The birds stopped singing, and everything was quiet.

I felt a little scared, but also very excited. I had never seen something like that before. My parents were smiling, and many people were clapping. It was a beautiful moment.

After that, the moon slowly moved away from the sun, and the light came back. The eclipse was over. The birds started to sing again, and people left the park.

It was a great day. I learned a lot about space, the sun, and the moon. While the eclipse was happening, we were experiencing something very special together. I will never forget that day.
