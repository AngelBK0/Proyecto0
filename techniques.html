Chat about feeling good and learning well

Hey there! Welcome to our little chat about feeling good and learning well. Think of this as a friendly check-in. Just go with the flow!

So, let's kick things off. Imagine this:

¿If you want to ace that next big exam, what’s the first thing you’ll do?

A) Will you probably obsessively study all night, right? (0 points)

B) Or maybe you'll break your study sessions into smaller chunks with little breaks in between? (2 points)

C) Perhaps you'll just hope for the best! (0 points)

Now, let's think about your body.

If you want to have more energy during the day, what small change will you likely make?

A) Will you skip breakfast because you're in a rush? (0 points)

B) Or will you try to eat something nutritious, even if it's just a piece of fruit? (2 points)

C) Maybe you'll just drink more coffee! (1 points)

Let's keep going.

If you feel overwhelmed with studying, what will you do to clear your head?

A) Will you just keep pushing through, no matter how stressed you feel? (0 points)

B) Or will you take a short walk or do something you enjoy for a little while? (2 points)

C) Perhaps you'll just scroll through social media for a "quick" break? (0 points)

Now, think about staying focused.

If you find yourself getting distracted easily while studying, what technique might you try?

A) Will you try to study in a noisy environment with lots of interruptions? (0 points)

B) Or will you find a quiet spot and maybe even turn off notifications on your phone? (2 points))

C) Maybe you'll just try to multitask! (0 points)

Result

Adding up the points from your answers, you got a total of

Between 6 and 8 points: Congratulations! It seems you have a good understanding of how to approach studying. Between 3 and 5 points: ¡Well done! You're on the right track in several areas. There are some strategies you're already implementing that are very beneficial. Between 0 and 2 points: Don't worry! This chat is precisely for exploring better ways to feel good and learn better.
