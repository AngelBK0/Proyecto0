The Maya Forest is a beautiful natural wonder in Central America. It is located in parts of Mexico, Guatemala, and Belize.

Natural Wonders
Let’s talk about amazing natural wonders. The Grand Canyon, Arizona: The Colorado River carved this massive crack over ages. Now huge, many admire its beauty, daily painted by the sun.
Great Barrier Reef
Great Barrier Reef, Australia: This massive ocean structure grew long ago. Tiny corals built it. Today, it is the largest living thing, home to many fish.
Mount Everest
Mount Everest, Himalayas: Earth's tallest. Tectonic plates pushed land up. After many tries, Hillary and Norgay summited in 1953. Climbers still face thin air and harsh weather for its famed view.
Amazon Rainforest
Amazon Rainforest, South America: It grew by the Amazon River and is home to immense biodiversity. Its trees grow tall. The rainforest helps global climate but faces deforestation. People work to protect it.
